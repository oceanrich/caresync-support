Frequently Asked Questions

🚀 How to get started with CareSync?

1. Download and install the CareSync app

2. On first launch, the app will intelligently detect your system language

3. Tap "Add Medication Reminder" to add your first medication

4. Set medication time and frequency, save to start receiving smart reminders

📸 How to use photo recognition feature?

1. Select "Photo Recognition" option when adding medication

2. Point camera at text information on medicine box

3. App will automatically recognize medication information

4. Confirm information is correct and save

🔒 Is my data secure?

Absolutely secure! CareSync uses complete local storage strategy:

• All medication data stored locally on your device

• OCR recognition completely processed locally

• No account registration required

• Your health privacy receives highest level protection
